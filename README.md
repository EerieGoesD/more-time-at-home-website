# 🏠 More Time At Home

**Holiday leave optimizer that maximizes your days off by smartly placing paid leaves around weekends and national holidays.**

🔗 **[Try it live](https://eeriegoesd.com/more-time-at-home/)** · ☕ [Buy Me a Coffee](https://buymeacoffee.com/eeriegoesd)

---

## What It Does

You have a limited number of paid leave days per year. This tool finds the **optimal placement** for each one, bridging weekends, national holidays, and custom days off, so you get the longest possible stretches of time at home.

## Hwo To
1. Pick your **year** and **country**
2. Set your number of **paid leaves** and optional **start date**
3. Adjust **constraints** if needed (e.g. "at least 10 days off in a row")
4. Click **🎯 Optimize**
5. Fine-tune by clicking individual days on the calendar
6. **💾 Save** your plan for later
