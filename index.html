Frequently Asked Questions

What is a holiday leave optimizer?

A holiday leave optimizer (also called a bridge day calculator or PTO maximizer) analyzes your country's public holidays and weekends to find the best dates to take paid leave. By placing leave days next to existing days off, you get the longest possible consecutive breaks from the fewest leave days.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs in your browser — no data is sent to any server. Holiday data comes from the open Nager.Date API, but your leave selections never leave your device.

Which countries are supported?

Currently 26 countries including the US, UK, Germany, France, Japan, Brazil, India, and more. Public holidays are fetched automatically. You can also add custom holidays manually by clicking any day on the calendar.

How does the optimization algorithm work?

The optimizer evaluates every possible placement of your leave days and scores each by efficiency — total consecutive days off divided by leave days used. It prioritizes placements that "bridge" gaps between weekends and holidays, giving you the most time off per leave day spent.

❓ How to Use

Getting Started

1. Pick the Year and Country — national holidays load automatically from the Nager.Date API.

2. Enter how many Paid Leaves you have per year.

3. Set Calculate From if you need to plan from a specific date.

Calendar Colors

■ White/Dark = Working day

■ Blue = National Holiday (from API)

■ Pink = Custom Holiday (forces a planned leave)

■ Green = Planned Leave (optimizer output)

■ Yellow = Weekend

■ Red = Holiday on weekend (wasted)

■ Brown = Working weekend (overridden)

Clicking Days

Click any day to cycle through states. Working days become national holidays → custom holidays → planned leaves → back to working. Weekend days toggle to working weekends. Planned leaves on weekends revert.

Constraints

Min X = optimizer only creates blocks with at least X consecutive days.

Max X = optimizer caps blocks at X consecutive days.

Days Off counts total consecutive days off (including weekends). Paid Leaves counts only actual leave days.

Optimize

Finds the best placement for your paid leaves to maximize total consecutive days off, working around weekends, national holidays, and custom holidays.

Save & Load

Save your plan as a .hplan file (JSON). Load it back anytime — works across sessions.
